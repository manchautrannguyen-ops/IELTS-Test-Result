Task Overview

Task: Scientists agree that many people eat too much junk food and it is damaging their health. Some people think that this problem can be solved by education, while others believe that this education will not work. Discuss both views and give your opinion.

Candidate's Essay

High junk food consumption has been confirmed detrimental to our well-being and many claim that this issue can be solved within an educational approach, while others do not agree so. Personally, education has both pros and cons in certain aspects.

On one hand, education about the effects of junk food can be transmitted via various means. One of the most popular ways might be early education for young children. At home, parents may educate them by preparing healthy diets and informing children with practical knowledge about what they consume daily. At schools, children have opportunities to get access to information about food through textbooks and science lessons. By this way, children will be able to develop a solid foundation of healthy diets positively affecting their eating habits at a very young age. Moreover, education may be conducted by raising public awareness. Governments and health organizations play an essential role in producing and promoting advertisements indicating consequent illnesses due to high consumption of junk food such as diabetes or heartstroke. Green campaigns can also be introduced in order to align the source of fiber and natural components to people's daily diets. This might lead to oily or processed food reduction and derivation of healthier food sources.

On the other hand, education may encounter certain difficulties which will not work properly. Fast food or processed food brands have been building images of their products as convenient, mouth-watering and made with fresh ingredients. Over a prolonged period, this has constructed a fixed notion of junk food in our mindsets, particularly the elderly people. In addition, the educational approaches seem to be theory-based and the consequences of junk food to our health may take time to occur. This, therefore, encourages the images of famous junk food brands and people will have a tendency to trust their tastes and visuals more than theoretical explanations.

From my perspective, junk food is becoming more and more multi-nationally widespread and is having a negative impact on our health. Education, despite their certain drawbacks, seems to be the key solution to this issue. From early diets education for children to mass awareness-raising activities, I strongly believe we may shape our eating habits into a healthier and beneficial version.

Detailed Evaluation Criteria

Criterion Score Comment
Task Response 6-7 The essay addresses both views and presents a personal opinion. There are some genuinely interesting and relevant ideas: the distinction between home education and school education, the role of advertising by food brands in creating fixed mindsets, and the theory-versus-practice gap in education are all thoughtful points. However, several issues limit the score. The introduction's framing (education has both pros and cons) is vague and does not reflect the actual debate, which is about whether education can solve the junk food problem. The second body paragraph's argument that education won't work because brand marketing is powerful is relevant, but the point about education being "theory-based" is underdeveloped and not clearly connected to the claim that it won't change behaviour. Some key questions to help you develop this idea: What is meant by theory-based? Why is theory-based education not effective in changing habits? What can be done to make education less theoretical and more practical? Also, be careful with the phrase "practical knowledge" in the first body paragraph as it seems contradictory to the point of theory-based education.
Coherence & Cohesion 7 There is a clear overall structure with logical progression from introduction through body to conclusion. Cohesive devices are used with reasonable variety: moreover, on the other hand, in addition, therefore, despite. Paragraph topic sentences are generally clear.
Lexical resource 6-7 There is a wider range of vocabulary than the lower-band essays, and the writer attempts less common items with some success: detrimental, prolonged period, mass awareness-raising, fixed notion. Collocation awareness is evident in places. However, there are noticeable errors and imprecisions:
• "heartstroke" (should be heart disease or stroke — "heartstroke" does not exist)
• "consequent illnesses" (resulting illnesses / illnesses that result from)
• "derivation of healthier food sources" (the intended meaning is unclear, perhaps adoption of healthier food sources)
• "informing children with practical knowledge" (providing children with)
Other unnatural phrases are emphasized in bold. These are recurring enough to limit precision but do not prevent communication overall.
Grammatical range & accuracy 6-7 The essay demonstrates a mix of simple and complex structures, and complex sentences are generally attempted with reasonable accuracy. Relative clauses, gerund phrases, and multi-clause sentences appear throughout. Key errors:
• "education may encounter certain difficulties which will not work properly" — the relative clause attaches to difficulties but the intended meaning is that education will not work, creating a logical mismatch.
• "the elderly people" should be the elderly or elderly people (not both).
• "High junk food consumption has been confirmed detrimental" requires confirmed to be detrimental or confirmed as detrimental.
These errors occur with enough frequency to be noticeable but generally do not impede understanding.

Cần làm gì để giáo dục bớt lý thuyết và mang tính thực hành hơn? Ngoài ra, cần cẩn thận với cụm "practical knowledge" ở đoạn body 1 vì nó có thể mâu thuẫn với lập luận "theory-based" ở body 2.
Coherence & Cohesion (Liên kết và mạch lạc) 7 Bài viết có cấu trúc tổng thể rõ ràng với mạch phát triển logic từ mở bài đến thân bài đến kết luận. Các từ nối được sử dụng khá đa dạng và hợp lý như moreover, on the other hand, in addition, therefore, despite. Câu chủ đề của các đoạn nhìn chung khá rõ ràng.
Lexical resource (Từ vựng và diễn đạt) 6-7 Phạm vi từ vựng rộng hơn so với các bài ở mức điểm thấp. Người viết có cố gắng sử dụng một số từ vựng ít phổ biến với mức độ thành công nhất định như detrimental, prolonged period, mass awareness-raising, fixed notion. Ở một số chỗ cũng cho thấy nhận thức khá tốt về collocation. Tuy nhiên, vẫn có những lỗi diễn đạt thiếu chính xác như:
• "heartstroke" (nên là heart disease hoặc stroke, không có từ "heartstroke")
• "consequent illnesses" (nên là resulting illnesses hoặc illnesses that result from)
• "derivation of healthier food sources" (nghĩa chưa rõ, có thể người viết muốn nói adoption of healthier food sources)
• "informing children with practical knowledge" (nên là providing children with)
Ngoài ra còn có một số cụm từ chưa tự nhiên khác. Những lỗi này xuất hiện đủ nhiều để làm giảm độ chính xác, nhưng nhìn chung vẫn không cản trở việc truyền tải ý nghĩa.
Grammatical range & accuracy (Ngữ pháp) 6-7 Bài viết sử dụng kết hợp cả câu đơn và câu phức, và các cấu trúc phức nhìn chung được sử dụng với độ chính xác tương đối. Mệnh đề quan hệ, cụm danh động từ và các câu nhiều mệnh đề xuất hiện xuyên suốt bài. Các lỗi chính:
• "education may encounter certain difficulties which will not work properly": mệnh đề quan hệ đang bổ nghĩa cho difficulties, trong khi ý định thực sự là education không hoạt động hiệu quả, tạo ra sự lệch logic.
• "the elderly people": nên là the elderly hoặc elderly people (không dùng cả hai cùng lúc).
• "High junk food consumption has been confirmed detrimental": nên là confirmed to be detrimental hoặc confirmed as detrimental.
Các lỗi này xuất hiện với tần suất đủ để được chú ý nhưng nhìn chung vẫn không cản trở việc hiểu ý.
